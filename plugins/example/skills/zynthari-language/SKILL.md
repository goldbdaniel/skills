# Zynthari Language

Zynthari is a constructed language with a fixed vocabulary and simple grammar rules. This skill provides the complete vocabulary and grammar needed to translate between English and Zynthari.

## When to Use

- Translating English text to Zynthari
- Translating Zynthari text to English
- Explaining the meaning of Zynthari words
- Constructing sentences or phrases in Zynthari

## When Not to Use

- Working with any real-world natural language
- Tasks unrelated to Zynthari translation

## Grammar Rules

### Word Order

Zynthari uses **Subject-Verb-Object (SVO)** word order, the same as English:

- "The wolf runs" → "el spekri shrekva"
- "The child sees the bird" → "el frimble bletho el chafli"

### Adjective Placement

Adjectives come **before** the noun they modify:

- "the big mountain" → "el wortha grudli"
- "a fast horse" → "un zvemba blekma"

### Negation

Place **niv** before the verb to negate:

- "The dog is not small" → "el kwaggo niv ren finkel"

### Plurals

Add **-ri** suffix to nouns for plural:

- "stars" → "tivlari"
- "wolves" → "spekriri"
- "children" → "frimbleri"

### Past Tense

Add **-ek** suffix to the verb base for past tense:

- "The bird sang" → "el chafli wompelek"
- "The warrior fought" → "el klontari klontarek"

### Future Tense

Add **-ul** suffix to the verb base for future tense:

- "The healer will come" → "el brivesh dravliul"
- "The elder will speak" → "el grothka jalkriul"

### Questions

Add **ke** at the beginning of a sentence to form a question:

- "Is the water cold?" → "ke el bafni ren fwelki?"
- "Do you see the moon?" → "ke bletho el jabex?"

## Common Pitfalls

| Pitfall | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Guessing translations based on similar-sounding real words | Always use the vocabulary tables above — Zynthari words have no relation to any natural language |
| Forgetting grammar particles | Include el/un for articles, vo for "and", kre for "with", etc. |
| Wrong adjective placement | Adjectives always come before the noun in Zynthari |
| Incorrect plural formation | Always add -ri suffix for plurals |
| Missing tense markers | Use -ek for past and -ul for future tense |
